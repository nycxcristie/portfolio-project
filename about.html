About Me

I am Cristina Isaacson. I graduated from Stony Brook University with a Bachelor of Science degree. I majored in Health Sciences (concentration in Health Care Management) and also minored in Studio Art.

After graduating, I traded my city life to live in the suburbs.... It was quite a humbling experience.

Shortly after getting married, I had children and became a stay-at-home mom. During this time, I experimented with baking and that became my side-gig. See that here

Couple years later, the pandemic hit and I became a small business owner on Etsy. From product design and package design, to manufacturing - I did it all. Etsy SEO keyword research, product photography and description, social media content-creation and marketing, responding to customer inquiries and tracking deliveries.... It was quite the learning curve, but within a year, I had made 644 sales and managed to recieve a 5-star average rating from 127 reviews.... most of them being strangers from all parts of the United States and quite many being repeat customers. See it here

My online business went through a transformation stage, where I got to sharpen the skills I learned in college. Slowly, people began reaching out to me for graphic design services for their personal and business related matters See them here and I thought how great it would be if I had the skills to do more. So I started SheCodes to become a front-end developer View coding projects here.
